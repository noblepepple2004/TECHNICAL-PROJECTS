# Case Study: AI-Assisted Engineering Study Workflow

## Objective

Reduce the time required to study complex engineering topics while maintaining accuracy and understanding.

## Problem

Engineering topics often involve lengthy textbooks and technical language that can be difficult to review efficiently before exams.

## Workflow

1. Identify the engineering topic.
2. Use AI to generate a structured explanation.
3. Verify the information with lecture notes and textbooks.
4. Rewrite the material into concise study notes.
5. Create practice questions for revision.

## Tools Used

- ChatGPT
- Microsoft Word

## Outcome

This workflow helped organize complex material into clear notes and made revision more efficient.

## Lessons Learned

AI is most effective when its responses are reviewed and verified rather than copied directly.
